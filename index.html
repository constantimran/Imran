About Us

A Mission Born from Compassion

Help Me to Help You was founded in 2018 by a group of community volunteers who saw firsthand how people in crisis often lacked not just resources — but someone who truly believed in them.

We believe that every person has untapped potential. Our role isn't to do things for people — it's to walk alongside them, remove barriers, and amplify their own power to create change. Together, we build communities where no one is left behind.

💛
Compassion First
Every interaction begins with empathy, dignity, and respect for the whole person.
🔍
Radical Transparency
We publish our finances openly and hold ourselves accountable to every donor and beneficiary.
🌱
Lasting Impact
We focus on sustainable change — not quick fixes — that outlasts our involvement.

Our Programs

What We Do

🏫

Education for All

We fund school supplies, tutoring, and scholarships for children from low-income families — because quality education should never depend on a zip code.

🍽️

Food Security Initiative

Through partnerships with local farms and food banks, we ensure that families facing food insecurity receive consistent, nutritious meals every week.

🏥

Community Health Access

We connect underserved individuals with free and low-cost healthcare, mental health support, and wellness education programs.

💼

Skills & Employment

Our vocational training and job-readiness workshops equip adults with marketable skills and direct pathways to meaningful employment.

🏠

Safe Housing Support

We help families access emergency housing assistance, rental support, and stable living environments to prevent homelessness.

🤝

Mentorship Network

We pair young people with experienced mentors who guide, inspire, and open doors — creating a cycle of giving that strengthens entire communities.
